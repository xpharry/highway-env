class highway_env.envs.parking_env.ParkingEnv(config=None)¶

Bases: highway_env.envs.common.abstract.AbstractEnv, gym.core.GoalEnv

A continuous control environment.

It implements a reach-type task, where the agent observes their position and velocity and must control their acceleration and steering so as to reach a given goal.

Credits to Munir Jojo-Verge for the idea and initial implementation.

step(action)¶

Perform an action and step the environment dynamics.

The action is executed by the ego-vehicle, and all other vehicles on the road performs their default behaviour for several simulation timesteps until the next decision making step.
Parameters:action (int) – the action performed by the ego-vehicle
Returns:a tuple (observation, reward, terminal, info)

Proximity to the goal is rewarded

  • desired_goal – the goal that was desired
  • info – any supplementary information
  • p – the Lp^p norm used in the reward. Use p<1 to have high kurtosis for rewards in [0, 1]
